Welcome to Time Wise. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit and use our Time Wise platform.

    We may collect the following types fo information:

    1. Personal Information

      • Full Name
      • Email Address
      • Phone Number
      • Employee ID
      • Account login credentials

    2. Work Activity Data

      • Check-in and check-out times
      • Total hours worked
      • Attendance status (e.g., full day, half day)

    3. Technical and Usage Data

      • IP Address
      • Browser and device information

  2. How We Use Your Informationn

    We use the inforamtion we collect for the following purpose:

    • To create and manage user accounts
    • To track employee attendance and work hours
    • To improve our platform's performance and user experience

    We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze platform performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

  4. Sharing og Information

    We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

    • With service providers (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)

  5. Data Security

    We implement reasonable security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your information. However no system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee 100% protection

  6. Your Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access and update your personal data
    • Delete your account
    • Request correction of inaccurate data

  7. Data Retention

    We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

  8. Children's Privacy

    Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

  9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page and the date of the latest revision will be updated above. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

Help & Support

Welcome to Help & Support

Thank you for using TIME WISE. This page is here to guide you through common questions, usage instructions, and ways to get help quickly. Whether you're checking in for work or managing your account, we're here to support you.

  1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    • Account & Login

      • How do I create an account?

        Go to the signup page, enter your details, and click register.

      • I forgot my password. How can i reset it?

        Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset it.

      • How do I chnage my email or profile info?

        Go to the profile / settings section after logging in.

    • Check-in / Check-Out

      • How do I check in and check out?

        After logging in, use the "Check In" and "Check Out" buttons on your dashboard.

      • What does "Half Day" mean?

        If you check out before 5:00 PM (the end of the shift), your status may be marked as "Half Day"

      • Can I check in late?

        Yes, but your status may show "Late" depending on your company's rules.

    • Attendance & Reports

      • How can I view my attendance report?

        Go to the "Reports" or "Attendance" section in your account.

      • Can I download my attendance data?

        Yes, there's a download button available in the report section.

      • Who can see my check-in data?

        Only you and authorized admins.
